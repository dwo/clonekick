Clonekick

A bookmarklet for adding similar events to Songkick quickly.

Adding many similar events (with the same lineup, or the same venue) to Songkick can get boring. This bookmarklet allows you to "clone" the details of the event you are viewing on Songkick into a new concert form.

Drag this onto your toolbar: clonekick.

Click the bookmarklet whilst viewing any concert on songkick.com to be taken to a new concert form with artists, venue, date and time copied into the input fields automatically.
